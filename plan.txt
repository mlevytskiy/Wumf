side menu:
- basic (uninstall/restore(from google play), move apps to sdcard, start app, find app, change list to grid with animation)
- social (social module - can see friend's apps)
- share app (can share link and apk via email https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.appshare.shrethis.appshare&hl=en)
- lock app (can lock app https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.domobile.applock&hl=en)
- backup (can backup and restore all apps with user data)
- hidden apps (show apps without launchers https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.seasmind.android.gmappmgr&hl=en)
